## 2. Strategic Partners (Pro Bono Services)

* **Goal:** To acquire professional services and expertise to run the campaign effectively without direct financial cost, while simultaneously strengthening my Leuven network.
* **Target:** Leuven-based companies and skilled professionals.
* **Profile:**
    * Market research / analysis firm
    * Advertisement / PR agency
    * Professional content creators (videographer, photographer)
    * Legal or administrative support
* **The Ask:** Pro bono services and expertise (not a financial contribution).
* **The Return:**
    * Official partner status and full visibility on the website and in campaign materials.
    * A unique and tangible way to support a local athlete.
    * Practical benefits (e.g., using a non-profit structure for services).
* **Rationale:** Keeping these partners local is practically easier and reinforces the "First Paralympian from Leuven" narrative by building a strong local support web.
